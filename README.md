🛒 Superstore Sales Analysis

Project Overview:

This project analyzes a Superstore sales dataset to extract business insights. Focus was on understanding sales trends, top-performing regions, and operational efficiency.

Key Features & Analysis:

-Cleaned and explored the dataset for missing or inconsistent data.
-Converted date columns and engineered time-based features.
-Analyzed yearly growth and monthly trends
-Identified top-performing regions, states, and product categories
-Determined most valuable customers and products
-Calculated shipping time performance
-Skills & Concepts Applied
-Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
-Date-Time Feature Engineering
-Grouping & Aggregation
-Business Metrics Analysis (Revenue, Orders, Customer Value)
-Operational Performance Tracking (Shipping time)

Key Takeaways:
-Gained practical experience with real-world business datasets
-Strengthened ability to extract insights for decision-making
-Improved understanding of Python, Pandas, and data-driven business analysis
